Family Law

At RME Solicitors, we specialize in providing compassionate, expert legal guidance in all areas of family law. Our dedicated team understands the emotional and legal complexities involved in family matters and is committed to supporting you through every step of your journey.



What We Do:
  • 1. Divorce and Separation: We assist clients in navigating the often challenging process of divorce and separation. Our goal is to achieve fair and amicable resolutions, minimizing stress and ensuring your interests are protected.
  • 2. Child Custody and Support: Ensuring the well-being of your children is our top priority. We work diligently to establish fair custody arrangements and child support agreements that reflect the best interests of your children.
  • 3. Spousal Support: Our experienced team helps clients negotiate and secure appropriate spousal support, ensuring financial stability and fairness post-separation or divorce.
  • 4. Property Division: We provide clear, strategic advice on the equitable division of marital assets and debts, helping you reach a fair settlement.
  • 5. Prenuptial and Postnuptial Agreements: Protect your future with our expertly crafted prenuptial and postnuptial agreements. We ensure your assets are safeguarded and your agreements are legally sound.
  • 6. Domestic Violence: Our compassionate team offers immediate and effective legal support for victims of domestic violence, helping secure protection orders and ensuring your safety.


Why Choose RME Solicitors?
  • Expertise and Experience: Our team of skilled family law attorneys brings years of experience and a deep understanding of family law to every case.
  • Compassionate Approach: We approach each case with sensitivity and care, recognizing the emotional toll family legal matters can take on our clients.
  • Personalized Service: We provide tailored legal solutions that meet your unique needs, ensuring you receive the best possible outcome.
  • Strong Advocacy: We are dedicated advocates for our clients, committed to protecting your rights and achieving fair resolutions.


At RME Solicitors, we are here to support you through life's most challenging transitions with integrity, expertise, and compassion. Let us guide you through your family law matters with the professionalism and care you deserve.
