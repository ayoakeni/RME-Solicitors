About US

RME Solicitors is a leading law firm specializing in family law, real estate law, and immigration law. With a team of experienced solicitors, RME Solicitors provides expert legal services to individuals and businesses across a wide range of legal matters.

RME Solicitors is a distinguished law firm renowned for its expertise in family law, real estate law, and immigration law. With a rich history of providing exceptional legal services, RME Solicitors has established itself as a trusted partner for individuals and businesses seeking comprehensive and personalized legal solutions.
Founded in September 2013, RME Solicitors has grown to become a leading legal firm known for its dedication to excellence and commitment to client satisfaction. Our team of experienced solicitors has successfully handled a wide range of cases, earning a reputation for delivering results with integrity and professionalism.

Family Law

Our family law solicitors are skilled in handling a variety of family-related legal matters, including divorce, child custody, and domestic violence cases. We understand the sensitive nature of family law issues and approach each case with compassion and understanding.

Real Estate Law

In the realm of real estate law, RME Solicitors offers comprehensive legal services for buying, selling, and leasing residential and commercial properties. Our real estate solicitors have a deep understanding of property law and are dedicated to protecting our clients' interests throughout the transaction process.

Immigration Law

Immigration law is a complex and ever-changing field, but our immigration solicitors are well-equipped to navigate its intricacies. Whether you are seeking to obtain a visa, apply for asylum, or secure citizenship, our team will provide you with expert guidance and representation.
